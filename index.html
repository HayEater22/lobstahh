The Lobster Cup International is an off-season FTC event located in Silicon Valley that attracts teams from all around the world. Each year, we expand the competition by welcoming more teams and elevating the gameplay. This year, the Lobster Cup will take place at the San Mateo Event Center on August 8–9.

The Lobster Cup is more than just an FTC event. All teams receive full access to the Robotics Fair, Silicon Valley’s premier interactive robotics and AI experience, featuring live technology demonstrations and industry showcases. Team registration is now open, so register your team to compete today!
